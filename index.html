About Me

I am an aspiring web developer living in the bay area, currently enrolled in UC Berkeley Extension's coding bootcamp and always looking for new ways to expand my coding repertoire. My interests include philosophy, technology, politics, and writing, but that's just to name a few, as I tend to find this world to be more than full of interesting (if terrible) things.
When I'm not honing my coding skills, I enjoy rock climbing at my local climbing gym, or getting outside to climb or hike whenever I can. I am also an avid PC gamer, and have enjoyed building my own PC for gaming and adding to my unreasonably large and still-growing library of games.
